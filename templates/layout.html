About Avevhox:


Want the sounds of the city without the hum of human voices? Perhaps you'd like to turn one of your favorite songs into an instrumental track?


Avevhox is a voice elimination application that lets you import .wav files and export them back out without the sound of the human voice. A project still in the making, Avevhox uses Hidden Markov Models to localize and erase human voices.


"Avevhox" is a portmanteau of the Latin words for "voice" and "elimination"


Made with Python, Flask, and Angular.JS.
